Children's Chess — 6×8

A gentler chess: a 6-wide board, six pawns, and just one bishop and one knight each. Rooks in the corners, queen on c, king on d. Each player picks which side their bishop stands.

Set up your bishops

Your lone bishop is stuck on one colour for the whole game. Put it on the b-file or the e-file — the knight takes the other square. Each player chooses on their own.

Promote pawn to…

Standard piece moves, two-step pawns, en passant, promotion at the far row. No castling yet. Draws are handled gently: you get a heads-up warning before a stalemate, and every draw (stalemate, threefold repetition, or too few pieces) shows who salvaged a draw and who squandered a win. Two players, one screen.
